The Stalker

Description

The Stalker is a fast-moving, ranged Protoss ground unit, capable of hitting both air and ground targets. They are warped in at the Gateway, and require a Cybernetics Core. The Stalker's name is indicative of its hit-and-run capabilities: they have a relatively high movement speed, and after the Blink upgrade has been researched at the Twilight Council, they can advance or retreat literally in a flash, bypassing rocks and hopping up and down cliffs.

Abilities

Blink

Teleports the Stalker to a nearby target location that is not obscured by Fog of War. Ability can only be used once every 7 seconds.

Features & Upgrades

Weapons Upgrade

Increase the Attack of Protoss ground units.
